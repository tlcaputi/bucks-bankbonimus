A 134-acre native-plant preserve and National Natural Landmark near Washington Crossing, with a Visitor Center offering exhibits, a gift shop, and educational programs plus miles of woodland trails. More nature center than museum, but its interpretive programming and proximity to the Washington Crossing sites fit the area.
