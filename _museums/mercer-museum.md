A six-story reinforced-concrete castle built 1913–1916 by archaeologist and collector Henry Chapman Mercer to house 40,000+ pre-industrial American tools and trade objects, with a whaleboat, stagecoach, and Conestoga wagon hung in the central atrium. A Smithsonian affiliate run by the Bucks County Historical Society.
