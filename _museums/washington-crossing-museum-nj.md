NJ-side state park marking Washington's Christmas-night 1776 crossing, directly across the river from New Hope. Its Visitor Center Museum (when open) houses the Swan Historical Foundation's 500+ Revolutionary War artifacts and the 'Ten Crucial Days' exhibit. Grounds are open, but the museum building is mid-reconstruction — confirm reopening before planning around it.
