A 12-room brick farmhouse (1810 core, expanded 1860s–1880s) on 126 acres of county parkland along the Delaware, with period furnishings, a Victorian barn, and exhibits on the Lenape who first inhabited the area. Run by Bucks County Parks & Recreation.
