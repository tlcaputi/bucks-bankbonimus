Delaware Canal State Park's first interpretive center, in a restored early-1800s locktender's cottage on New Hope's Main Street. 'Life at the Lock' exhibits show how mule-drawn boats and lock-keepers worked the canal 1832–1932; it also serves as HQ for the Friends of the Delaware Canal. Pairs well with a towpath stroll.
