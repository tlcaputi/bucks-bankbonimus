The eclectic 44-room hand-poured concrete home Henry Chapman Mercer built for himself (1908–1912), with his decorative tiles and prints embedded directly into the walls, ceilings, and floors, plus 6,000+ books in built-in cases. Operated by the Bucks County Historical Society, a short walk from the Mercer Museum along the 'Mercer Mile.'
