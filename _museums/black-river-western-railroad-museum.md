A small railroad museum in a vintage Central Railroad of NJ baggage car (#420) parked beside the Ringoes station of the Black River & Western heritage line. Displays and an operating model railroad cover the volunteer-run line and Hunterdon County rail history — a natural add-on for anyone riding the excursion train.
