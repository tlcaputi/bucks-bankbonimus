Oscar Hammerstein II's Bucks County farm (1940–1960), where he wrote lyrics for Oklahoma!, South Pacific, and more. A nonprofit is restoring the property; 45-minute guided tours cover his legacy, but access is limited to scheduled, pre-ticketed dates.
