A 1702 farmhouse that served as a Continental Army field hospital before Washington's 1776 crossing, plus a working 1830s water-powered grist mill on the same farmstead. Revolutionary War history in a rural setting, accessible only via a timed, pre-booked guided tour that runs seasonally.
