An 1816 Federal brick home, boyhood home of James Wilson Marshall, whose 1848 gold discovery in California sparked the Gold Rush. Now HQ of the Lambertville Historical Society with period rooms and local-history exhibits, a few minutes from New Hope over the river bridge. Also the start point for the Society's guided walking tours.
