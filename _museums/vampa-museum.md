Self-described 'largest vampire and paranormal museum in the world,' on a former dairy farm on Route 202 north of Doylestown alongside a large antiques barn. Collection spans 1700s vampire-hunting kits, exorcism artifacts, Ouija boards, and Dracula-film memorabilia.
