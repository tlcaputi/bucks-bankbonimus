Also known as the Phineas Pemberton House, a group of connected structures built 1687–1790 — among the earliest architecture in Bucks County — that later served as the Levitt & Sons headquarters during the building of Levittown. The Friends of Bolton Mansion give first-floor tours by appointment plus periodic open houses.
