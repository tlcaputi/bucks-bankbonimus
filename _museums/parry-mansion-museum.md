An 18th-century (1784) merchant's mansion built by New Hope founder Benjamin Parry, now a house museum run by the New Hope Historical Society. Rooms are furnished to show 125 years (1775–1900) of the family's changing tastes via short guided tours. A quick, walkable stop right on Main Street.
