An 1865 one-room schoolhouse in Solebury Village serving as the township historical society's HQ, archive, and small museum of local artifacts, photos, and maps spanning 200+ years. Best for house-history and genealogy research, though the schoolhouse itself is worth a quick look during its limited open hours.
