Working studio and National Historic Landmark complex built by master woodworker George Nakashima, now run by his daughter Mira; buildings, furniture, and grounds are largely as he left them. A must for design/craft fans, but NOT a walk-in museum — the free Saturday open house has ended, so visiting needs an appointment or a reserved Foundation tour seat.
