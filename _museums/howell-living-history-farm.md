A working farm museum run by the Mercer County Park Commission recreating rural NJ farm life circa 1890–1910, with draft horses, heritage-breed animals, and costumed interpreters. Walk-in self-guided visiting is standard; seasonal programs like Corn Harvest and Christmas on the Farm add weekend hours.
