A restored 1880s house at the entrance to Clawson Memorial Park, furnished to depict 1880–1920 life — Victorian bedroom, period living room, and a 1920s kitchen with icebox and Hoosier cabinet. Covers East Amwell / Ringoes / Reaville history.
