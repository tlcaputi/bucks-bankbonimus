A weekday visitor-center museum plus two historic buildings toured by appointment: the 1812 four-story Burgess-Foulke House and 1772 Liberty Hall, where the Liberty Bell rested Sept 23, 1777 en route to Allentown. At the very edge of the 45-minute radius.
