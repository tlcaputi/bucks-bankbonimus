A water-powered gristmill built in 1800 and worked until 1955, on Tohickon Creek in Tinicum Park. Most original millworks (four sets of millstones, two rare metal turbines) remain in place, with a second-floor museum of 19th/early-20th-century milling and canal-trade tools. 'Milling Days' let visitors watch the machinery run.
