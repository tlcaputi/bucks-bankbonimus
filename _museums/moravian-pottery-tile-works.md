A working history museum built 1911–1912 by Henry Chapman Mercer to produce his handcrafted Arts & Crafts mosaic tiles, still made on-site by hand using Mercer's original methods. Owned by Bucks County government and operated by the nonprofit TileWorks of Bucks County — the third stop on the 'Mercer Mile.'
