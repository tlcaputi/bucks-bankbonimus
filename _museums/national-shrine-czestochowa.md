A major Polish-American Catholic pilgrimage site on Beacon Hill (the highest point in Bucks County), run by the Pauline Fathers; the main shrine was dedicated in 1966. Visitor-facing components include a museum exhibition, outdoor Stations of the Cross, a Polish-American cemetery, gift shop, and Polish deli. Primarily a religious shrine rather than a conventional museum.
