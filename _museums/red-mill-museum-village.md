An iconic red 19th-century grist/woolen mill on the South Branch Raritan, now a village of about a dozen historic structures (general store, one-room schoolhouse, log cabin). One of NJ's most-photographed landmarks, a short walk from the Hunterdon Art Museum. On the farther edge of the radius.
